Eileen's Emotions

Meet Joy: She is the first emotion to manifest itself in Eileen's head and does her best to help her human girl make happy memories.

Meet Angry: He does his best to protect Eileen from injustices like no dessert.

Meet Fear: He is on a constant lookout for every possible danger that could befall Eileen whether it's the first day of school or nightmarish clowns.

Meet Sadness: None of the emotions, not even Sadness herself, understand why she exists.

Meet Disgust: She is always looking out for Eileen's best interests like making sure she doesn't wear tacky clothes or get poisoned by the mysterious green substance known as broccoli (yuck!).
